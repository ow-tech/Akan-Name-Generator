AKAN-Names

This is a web based application that generates a user name depending on their day of birth,
and assigns the user an AkAN name.
The user has to provide correct dates for the application to work

The Naming System

The Akan people from Ghana have a system of naming their children basing on the day of the week that they are born. Each and evry child bears a Names derived from the day that they were born.
